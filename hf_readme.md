# Insurance Re-Shopping Predictor

Predicts whether you'd benefit from re-shopping your car insurance — using ML trained on 381K real insurance profiles.

**[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Archit-Konde/insurance-reshopping-predictor)**

## Quick Start

1. Enter your insurance profile in the left panel
2. Click **Predict** to get your re-shopping probability score
3. View SHAP explanations showing which factors drive your score
4. Check the **Data Quality Report** tab for dataset validation

## Tech Stack

Python, LightGBM, SHAP, Streamlit, scikit-learn, pandas
